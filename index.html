A Genomic Study on Dyslexia

 DYX1C1 (dyslexia susceptibility 1 candidate 1) is the very first gene to be found linked with Dyslexia. Dyslexia is a common problem that kids and parents don't realize. Many dyslexic kids in China are thought as stupid or not motivated. This page investigates in the molecular reasons of DYX1C1 causing dyslexia and hope to bring awareness of dyslexia.
