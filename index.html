About Me

Hi! I am Yukun Li. I am a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in Mobile and IoT Engineering. Previously, I graduated from the University of Wisconsin - Madison double majoring in Computer Science and Genetics. I am passionate about creating mobile and IoT technologies to help people live better lives. I am particularly interested in digital health and its potential in creating accessible, preventative, and personalized care. Aside from work, I enjoy volunteering, traveling, biking, learning languages, cooking, and reading.

I hope to use this space to share things I am passionate about.

A Genomic Study on Dyslexia

 DYX1C1 (dyslexia susceptibility 1 candidate 1) is the very first gene to be found linked with Dyslexia. This website introduces possible molecular reasons of DYX1C1 causing dyslexia and presents an original experiemnt design on navigating the relationship of creativity and dyslexia. I hope to use this page to bring awareness to potentials of people who have dyslexia.
